# Breakfast smoothie

## Servings: 
1 serving per smoothie

## Time to prepare: 
10 minutes

## Ingredients: 
1 banana, 1/2 cup blueberries, 1/4 cup yogurt, 4 cups of milk, 1 scoop protein powder, 1-gallon water

## Equipment needed: 
1 blender

## Steps for preparation: 
combine all ingredients in blender and blend together
